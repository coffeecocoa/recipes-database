The best steak marinade (or Tri Tip marinade)! This easy, flavorful marinade recipe can be used on any type of animal protein for any cooking method!

image: https://www.theroastedroot.net/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/best_steak_marinade_2.jpg 

# Ingredients

- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1.5 Tbsp fresh ginger, peeled and grated
- 3 Tbsp coconut sugar
- 1/2 cup coconut aminos, or liquid aminos or soy sauce
- 1/4 cup rice vinegar
- 1/4 cup broth, or water
- 1 Tbsp sesame oil
- 2 Tbsp avocado oil
- 1 tsp fish sauce, optional
- 1 Tbsp sriracha, more or less to taste
- 1 sprig fresh rosemary, or 2 tsp dried rosemary

# Instructions

1. Add all of the ingredients for the marinade to a blender and blend until well-combined. Note: the marinade may separate...this is normal and not a cause for concern.

2. Transfer the marinade to a sealable jar and refrigerate until ready to use. Store in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.

3. Before using marinade, shake it well (or blend it again) until it appears well-combined.
